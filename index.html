What is Technical Analysis(TA)?

Technical Analysis is a method that is used to predict the probable future price movement of a stock or currency pair.
In any kinds of trading, a wise trader should have an entry and exit strategy.
Using technical indicators can help you make better decisions on when to place a trade, when to exit a trade and when to stay away from the market (no trade is also a trade).

Candlestick Chart


Candlestick charts are commonly used by traders to determine the possible price movements based on previous price actions and patterns.
It's useful because it shows four price points: open, close, high and low throughout the period of time in which the trader selects.

Styles of Trading

There are different styles of trading, for example:

Day trader opens and closes a trade or multiple trades within the same day.
Swing traders can take a trade that lasts a couple of days up or months before they take profit from an anticipated price move.
Scalp traders specializes in profiting off of small price changes and making a fast profit off reselling, the aim is to keep compounding the small profits until it turns into a big gain.
Position trading is a long-term trading style where trades are kept open for months or years.
The difference between position trading and investing is that position traders have a stop-loss and take-profit target (just like any trader should have).
Investors adopt a buy and hold strategy.

With trading comes risk management and it is very important that traders practice this.
It is why technical analysis is used; to give the trader a better insight into where the price action is going and to plan for an entry and/or exit strategy.
