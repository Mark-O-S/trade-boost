About Us

We are Trade Boost

Our purpose is to teach and help people to trade the market, whether it be in the stock market, cryptocurrency or the forex market.
The fundamental principles of technical analysis applies to any financial markets.

Founders

From what started inside of an online forum chat. Like-minded traders shared their knowledge and were publishing their ideas online in an online forum until a suggestion came up to create a website to teach people how to trade.
Carl Tahn and Amir Khan were amongst these traders. Together, after testing proof of concepts, trial and error. They created Trade Boost. Fast forward 5 years later, Trade Boost has helped to create thousands of profitable traders and gain financial freedom.

Co-founder: Carl Tahn

Carl is a swing trader and mainly trades in the crypto market. Originally from Hong Kong, he moved to America to study business but dropped out of college on his final year to become a full time trader. In 5 years of trading and chatting in online forums he made contact and eventually partnered up with Amir to develop Trade Boost as we know it today.

Co-founder: Amir Khan

Amir was born and raised in Brighton, England. He moved to study in London, graduating with a degree in Finance. Amir was working for one of the biggest banks in England and trading part time. Amir quickly fell in love with trading in the stock market. After a few years of working in finance, Amir transitioned out and teamed up with Amir to create Trade Boost.
